Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to your most common questions about our plans and features.

1. What are the advantages of planning a trip using this AI model with respect to using Chat GPT or similar?

Our Chat Bot is trained specifically for travel purpose, and is always considering real-time events (such as strikes, delays, opportunities...) in the planning of activities.

2. How does real-time event information work?

We use AI and web scraping to gather real-time event updates, including changes in schedules, weather conditions, and local activities. This ensures that you always have the most current information to help you plan your trip on the go.

3. What’s the difference between the Free, Premium, and Enterprise Plans?

The Free Plan offers access to basic guides for popular destinations, while the Premium Plan gives you unlimited access to personalized itineraries and real-time event updates. The Enterprise Plan provides tailored itineraries for businesses and frequent travelers, along with dedicated support and exclusive local partnerships.

4. What kind of support do I get with each plan?

Free Plan users have access to basic support, available during business hours. Premium Plan users get priority support with quicker response times, and Enterprise Plan users enjoy 24/7 dedicated customer support for all their travel needs.

5. Can I upgrade or cancel my plan later on?

Yes, you can easily upgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. Once upgraded, you’ll gain immediate access to the new features and benefits that come with the higher-tier plan. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.

TouristAI University Project

TouristAI is a non-commercial project developed by an international team of university students as part of an academic course focused on high tech entrepreneurship and digital innovation.

This website is intended exclusively for educational and research purposes. The features, content, and services displayed here are prototypes and do not represent a commercial offering. No travel bookings or paid services are provided.

All data shown is either fictional or used under fair use for academic demonstration. TouristAI serves as a proof of concept to explore how generative AI can enhance personalized travel experiences.
