TouristAI is a non-commercial project developed by an international team of university students as part of an academic course focused on high tech entrepreneurship and digital innovation.

This website is intended exclusively for educational and research purposes. The features, content, and services displayed here are prototypes and do not represent a commercial offering. No travel bookings or paid services are provided.
